- You can zoom out to see patterns

The semantic understanding and the mathematical proof are identical — just viewed at different resolutions. Translating between them is a matter of "zooming," not of deriving something new.

This is the semantic proof. It expresses *why* the result must hold.

### 3.4 Step 4: Scale Down

Increase resolution step by step, maintaining the same truth:
